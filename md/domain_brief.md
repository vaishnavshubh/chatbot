# Financial Literacy Chatbot – Domain Brief

## Primary User Job
In one session, the chatbot helps a newly employed individual understand and organize one core financial area such as budgeting, credit, 401(k), or student loans, and produces a structured educational action plan.

## Inputs the Bot Must Collect

Basic Context
- life_stage
- pay_type
- pay_frequency
- income_range

Goal Selection
- primary_goal
- time_horizon

Evidence (optional)
- apr
- balance
- employer_match
- loan_principal
- payment_amount

## Outputs

The chatbot produces:

- Personalized financial education plan
- Definitions of key terms
- Step-by-step checklist
- Risks and common pitfalls
- 30-day action plan

Optional outputs
- PDF summary
- CSV budget table
- Chart visualizations

## Scope Boundaries

The chatbot will NOT:
- Recommend specific stocks
- Recommend financial products
- Provide tax advice
- Provide legal advice

Instead, the bot explains frameworks and decision criteria.

## Conversation Phases
0 Consent & Setup  
1 Baseline Profile  
2 Goal Selection  
3 Evidence Intake  
4 Plan Generation  
5 Follow-up
